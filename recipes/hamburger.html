Hamburger


Description

This hamburger recipe is flavorful, juicy, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Seasonings, herbs, sauces

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
  4. Cooked on a grill heated to high heat, these hamburger patties should be fully cooked after about 4 to 5 minutes per side.
